About Project 208

Project 208 is dedicated to bridging the educational gap in underprivileged rural communities by providing accessible and engaging STEM learning opportunities. We believe that every young mind, regardless of their location or background, deserves the chance to explore the wonders of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Our focus is on equipping young girls with practical skills, fostering innovation, and inspiring them to become problem-solvers in their own communities. Through hands-on projects like building wind turbines and generators, we aim to ignite a lifelong passion for STEM and empower the next generation of rural innovators.
